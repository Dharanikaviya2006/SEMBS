## Tips

- **Logo Size**: The logo is automatically sized to 15% of your image width
- **Caption**: Appears below the image, not on the canvas
- **Hashtags**: Automatically added: #he5solutions #itconsulting #remodeling
- **Quality**: Your photos maintain their original resolution
- **Privacy**: Everything runs in your browser - no uploads to servers

## Need Your Own Logo?

1. Replace `he5logo.svg` with your own logo file
2. Name it `he5logo.png` or `he5logo.svg`
3. Refresh the page - your logo will appear automatically!

See `LOGO_GUIDE.md` for detailed instructions.

## Deploy to the Web

Want to share this tool with your team?

1. Create a free GitHub account
2. Upload these files to a new repository
3. Enable GitHub Pages in repository settings
4. Get a public URL to share!
